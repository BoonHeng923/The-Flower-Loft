Floral Design Workshop

Discover the elegance of floral design with our hands-on flower arranging workshop.
Open to all flower enthusiasts, regardless of experience, our Floral Design Workshops invite you to explore the art of floral design.

Beginner's Workshop

This workshop is perfect for those new to the world of floral design. Participants will learn the basics of selecting and conditioning flowers, understanding color combinations, and creating simple yet beautiful arrangements.

RM 300

Advanced Workshop

For those with a foundational knowledge of floral arrangement, this advanced workshop delves into more complex techniques. Participants will explore floral sculpting, use of floral foam, and designing for large events or installations.

RM 450

Creative Expression

The workshop provides a creative outlet, allowing participants to experiment with various flowers, colors, and design elements. This creative freedom helps individuals discover their unique style and artistic voice in floral design.

Hands-On Learning

Participants receive direct, hands-on instruction from experienced florists, ensuring they grasp essential techniques and tips. Such immersive experiences are invaluable for building confidence and proficiency in floral arranging.

Stress Relief

Floral arranging can be a therapeutic activity, offering a calming and meditative experience. Working with flowers and natural elements helps participants disconnect from daily stresses and focus on the present moment. This aspect of the workshop contributes to overall well-being, promoting relaxation and mental clarity.
